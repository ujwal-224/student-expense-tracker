Track your daily expenses and finally understand where your money actually goes. By recording what you spend each day, you build awarness of your habits and avoid end-of-month surprises. Managing money as a student doesn't have to be confusing.

Log your spending in seconds and see clear summaries by category such as food, travel, entertainment and many more. Visual breakdowns help you understand which areas consume most of your budget. Spot habits, avoid overspending, and make smarter choices every month
